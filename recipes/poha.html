Recipe for Poha

Description:

If you are looking for an easy breakfast idea, you must try this Poha Recipe. Poha is a popular Indian breakfast made with flattened rice, tempered with fresh curry leaves, onions, veggies, peanuts and lemon juice.

The best place to find ultimate delicious Poha is Indore. It is their traditional breakfast and a perfect street food. It has a mix of savory and sweet flavors. If you love spicy, you might skip mild sweet Namkeens that are used for garnishing the Poha.

Ingredients:

  • Poha
  • Oil
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Jeera
  • Hing
  • Curry Leaves
  • Potato, sliced thinly
  • Green Chillies
  • Roasted Peanuts
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Coriander (for garnish)
  • Bhujia (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Heat Oil in a Pan
  2. Put Mustard Seeds, Jeera, Hing and Curry Leaves once oil is hot
  3. Put in the Potatoes and cook till slightly brown
  4. Put in the Green Chillies and Roasted Peanuts and cook for a few seconds
  5. Add the Turmeric Powder, Red Chilli Powder, Salt and Poha and mix
  6. Turn off the flame, add Sugar and Lemon Juice, mix and then cover with a lid for a few minutes
  7. Garnish with Coriander and Bhujia and Enjoy!
